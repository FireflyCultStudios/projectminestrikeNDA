1. Parties

Disclosing Party: Firefly Cult Studios (the Studio), the developer and owner of the software product currently designated MineStrike (internal working title; final release title withheld), together with all associated materials, concepts, and intellectual property.

Receiving Party: (the Tester), operating under callsign , who has applied to participate in the closed pre-alpha testing programme.

This Agreement is entered into as of .

2. Confidential Information

For the purposes of this Agreement, Confidential Information means all non-public information disclosed by the Studio to the Tester in connection with the pre-alpha testing programme, whether disclosed orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to:

  • The software, source code, build files, and executable(s) provided for testing.
  • The game's title, branding, artwork, audio, user interface, and design documents.
  • Gameplay mechanics, rules, systems, modes, and unreleased features.
  • Bug reports, feedback, test data, and internal communications relating to development.
  • Business plans, release dates, pricing, and any other commercial information.
  • The identity of other testers or members of the testing group.
3. Tester's Obligations

The Tester agrees to:

  • Keep all Confidential Information strictly private. The Tester shall not disclose, share, publish, or communicate any Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Studio.
  • No streaming or recording. The Tester shall not livestream, screen-record, capture, upload, or otherwise broadcast any gameplay footage, screenshots, or audio derived from the pre-alpha build on any platform, including but not limited to Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, Discord, Twitter/X, Reddit, or any other social media or video-sharing service.
  • No discussion outside approved test groups. The Tester shall not discuss, describe, or reference the game's content, mechanics, title, or development status in any public forum, community, or platform outside of the designated and approved testing channels provided by the Studio.
  • No copying or distribution. The Tester shall not copy, distribute, reverse-engineer, decompile, or modify any part of the software or materials provided.
  • Secure handling. The Tester shall take reasonable steps to keep the build and all associated materials secure and shall not permit any other person access to them.
  • Prompt notification. The Tester shall notify the Studio immediately upon becoming aware of any unauthorised disclosure or use of Confidential Information.
4. Exclusions

The obligations in clause 3 do not apply to information that the Tester can demonstrate:

  • Was already publicly known at the time of disclosure through no fault of the Tester.
  • Becomes publicly known after disclosure through no act or omission of the Tester.
  • Was independently developed by the Tester without reference to the Confidential Information.
  • Is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the Tester gives the Studio prompt prior written notice to allow the Studio to seek a protective order.
5. Duration

This Agreement comes into force on the date signed by the Tester and remains in full force and effect until the Studio makes an official public release announcement of the game (under its final release title or otherwise), at which point the obligations in clause 3 shall cease with respect to information that forms part of the public release. Obligations relating to information that remains non-public (including unreleased content, source code, and internal materials) continue indefinitely beyond public release.

6. Termination

The Studio may terminate the Tester's participation in the testing programme at any time, at its sole discretion, with or without cause. Upon termination, the Tester's obligations under clauses 3, 5, and 7 shall continue in full force and effect.

7. Return of Materials

Upon request by the Studio, or upon termination of the Tester's participation in the testing programme for any reason, the Tester shall promptly delete all copies of the software, builds, and associated materials from all devices and storage media in their possession or control, and confirm such deletion to the Studio in writing within 7 days of the request.

8. Intellectual Property

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Tester any licence, right, title, or interest in or to the Confidential Information or any intellectual property of the Studio. All intellectual property rights in MineStrike and all associated materials remain the sole and exclusive property of Firefly Cult Studios.

9. Feedback

Any feedback, suggestions, bug reports, or ideas provided by the Tester to the Studio in connection with the testing programme (Feedback) may be used freely by the Studio without restriction, compensation, or attribution. The Tester assigns to the Studio all rights in any such Feedback to the extent permitted by law.

10. Remedies

The Tester acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause the Studio irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate. The Studio shall be entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief in addition to any other remedy available at law or in equity, without the requirement to post a bond or other security.

11. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Each party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales to settle any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

12. General
  • Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, or understandings.
  • Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
  • No Waiver. Failure to enforce any provision shall not constitute a waiver of future enforcement.
  • Amendments. This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
  • Electronic Signatures. The parties agree that electronic signatures, whether digital or otherwise, shall be deemed valid and binding for all purposes of this Agreement, with the same force and effect as original ink signatures.
